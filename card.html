Pit Bull Dogs

VetStreet.com has ranked the American Pit Bull Terrier as one of the top three favorite dog breeds in the US!

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus Familiaris
  • Average Length: 20 Inches
  • Average Lifespan: 13 Years
  • Habitat: Households

The Pitbull can be traced back to the early 1800's in the United Kingdom and became known as the "All American Dog," in the USA.
